If you're trying to work out what a gutter clean should cost in Melbourne, the short answer is this: **single storey homes generally run between $150 and $220, and double storey between $220 and $320.**

What moves the price up or down is worth understanding before you book.

## Typical Price Ranges

| Property type | Price range |
|---|---|
| Single storey | $150 – $220 |
| Double storey | $220 – $320 |

Prices vary based on property size, roof configuration and access. We provide a firm quote before any work starts.

## What Affects the Price

**Property size** is the main driver. A compact single storey home with standard eave gutters and straightforward access is a simpler job than a large double storey on a sloping block with restricted side access.

**Roof type** matters too. Homes with valleys — the internal corners where two roof sections meet — accumulate debris faster and take longer to clear. Properties with flat or low-pitch sections, common in newer SE Melbourne estate builds, often have internal box gutters that require more careful clearing than standard eave gutters.

**Access** is the third factor. A ground-floor gutter on a flat block is straightforward. The same gutter on a steep block where the eave sits at second-storey height is a different proposition. Restricted access — locked side gates, no clearance for ladders — adds time and affects pricing.

## What's Typically Included

A standard professional gutter clean should cover:

- Full debris clearance from all gutters and valleys
- Downpipe flushing
- Basic roofline inspection
- Written job report on completion

Some operators include a written report; some don't. It's worth confirming what's included when you get a quote.

**What shouldn't be in a standard quote:** structural repairs, re-securing loose gutters, downpipe replacements. These are separate jobs that cost more and require specific trades.

## SE Melbourne Pricing Context

Across Berwick, Cranbourne, Officer, Clyde North and surrounding suburbs, pricing sits in line with the Melbourne-wide averages above.

- **Established leafy suburbs** (Berwick, Narre Warren South) — often have heavier debris loads on first cleans if they haven't been maintained regularly, which can affect time and cost
- **Newer estates** (Clyde North, Officer) — generally straightforward jobs given limited tree coverage, but box gutter designs and flat roof sections on contemporary builds can add complexity

## Getting a Quote

Price ranges are useful for budgeting, but the only accurate number is the one based on your specific property. A reputable operator will quote before starting work — not present a bill when the job is done.
